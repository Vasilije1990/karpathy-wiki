# Andrej Karpathy Wiki

This is a markdown-first, Cognee-backed knowledge wiki about Andrej Karpathy's public work. The wiki is designed to compound: sources become pages, queries become reusable synthesis, feedback becomes page and skill improvement.

The website has a live `Ask Cognee` tab when `scripts/wiki_server.py` is running: it recalls Cognee memory, searches the markdown wiki, and files reviewed query-synthesis pages automatically.

## Current Thesis

Synthesis: Karpathy's public work repeatedly turns advanced AI systems into teachable, inspectable artifacts. The through-line across courses, essays, repositories, and notes is not only building models, but making their mechanics legible enough that other people can reproduce and modify them.
